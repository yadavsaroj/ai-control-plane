# AI-Native Engineering Playbook

This playbook will define practical engineering patterns for building, operating, and improving AI-native systems.

## Initial Principles

- Treat prompts, policies, evaluations, and model choices as production artifacts.
- Design for traceability across inputs, decisions, tools, and outputs.
- Keep humans accountable for policy, risk, and escalation paths.
- Make quality measurable through continuous evaluation.
- Prefer composable platform primitives over one-off integrations.
