# AI-Native Engineering Principles

## 1. Architecture Before Implementation

Implementation becomes inexpensive.
Architecture becomes the scarce resource.

## 2. AI Is a Collaborator, Not an Owner

Humans own engineering judgment.
AI accelerates execution.

## 3. Interfaces Are More Valuable Than Implementations

Implementations will evolve.
Interfaces become long-lived contracts.

## 4. Small, Well-Defined Tasks Produce Better Outcomes

Context matters more than prompt engineering.

## 5. Review Is More Valuable Than Typing

Engineering leaders create leverage through review and architectural decisions.
