# Purpose

The AI Control Plane is a long-term exploration of the architecture, principles, and engineering practices required to operate production AI systems at enterprise scale.

Unlike a traditional product roadmap, this document focuses on the evolution of ideas, architectural capabilities, and reference implementations rather than feature delivery.

The roadmap is organized into milestones, each building on the previous one.

---

# Milestone 1 - Foundation

**Objective**

Establish the intellectual foundation of the AI Control Plane.

### Deliverables

* Vision
* Repository structure
* Reference architecture overview
* Initial architecture proposals
* AI-native engineering workflow
* Research notebook

### Success Criteria

* Clearly define the AI Control Plane concept.
* Establish architectural scope and non-goals.
* Build a common vocabulary for future work.

# Long-Term Vision

The long-term goal of this initiative is not to build another AI platform.

It is to contribute to a shared understanding of how production AI systems should be operated.

Success is measured not by GitHub stars or lines of code, but by the quality of the architectural thinking, the usefulness of the reference implementation, and the conversations this work enables within the engineering community.

If this initiative helps platform teams think more clearly about operating AI systems at scale, it will have achieved its purpose.

---

# Guiding Principles

Throughout every milestone, this initiative will prioritize:

* First-principles thinking over trends.
* Architecture before implementation.
* Working software over theoretical diagrams.
* Platform capabilities over application features.
* Operational excellence over feature velocity.
* Teaching and knowledge sharing over project size.

Every artifact in this repository should contribute to one overarching goal:

> **Define the operational layer for production AI systems.**
